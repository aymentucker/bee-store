## How to Replace Images

To replace images with your own:

1. Open `/src/data/categories.ts` and `/src/data/products.ts`
2. Replace the `imageUrl` or `images` array URLs with your own image URLs
3. You can use:
   - Local images in the `/public` folder (e.g., `/images/product1.jpg`)
   - External URLs from your own hosting
   - Other free stock photo services
